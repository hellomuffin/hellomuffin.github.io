VirtualHome Benchmark · 18 episodes

9 Unity-renderable task families × {easy, hard}. Each episode pairs a natural-language instruction with a rendered execution and the action scene graph composited at the bottom (the current action highlighted as it plays). Easy vs hard differ by room-object complexity and rooms traversed; every hard episode traverses ≥3 rooms. Hardness score = number of edges in the action scene graph.
